About Your Host

Dr. Christopher Butler

Licensed Acupuncturist & Functional Medicine Practitioner

Chris Butler came to Chinese medicine after watching the same pattern repeat in patients: people doing everything "right" on paper—eating well, taking supplements, following protocols—still getting sick, still run down, still frustrated.

Chinese medicine gave him a different lens. One that looks at stress load, nervous system tone, recovery capacity, digestion, sleep, and how the body responds under pressure. Over time, he saw patients stop being the one who always got sick and start becoming the person everyone else wondered about.

With decades of clinical practice and deep study in both Chinese and functional medicine, Chris brings a calm, practical approach to understanding health that makes sense—no fear-based messaging, no medical jargon, just clear explanations of how to build real physiological resilience.
