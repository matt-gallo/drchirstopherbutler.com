Our Practice

Butler Acupuncture Center in Cranford

A small, calm clinic built for patients who want thoughtful answers, evidence-informed care, and the tools to guide themselves between visits.

What we stand for

  • Education before prescription—so you understand your physiology.
  • Integrated care plans that blend acupuncture, neuro support, and lifestyle work.
  • Simple communication: phone, text, community, or in-person follow ups.
  • Respect for your time with streamlined scheduling and minimal wait lists.

Clinic snapshot

Location: 301 North Ave East, Cranford, NJ

Schedule: Tuesdays & Thursdays in person + telemedicine support during the week.

Patients: Adults navigating brain fog, hormone shifts, immune issues, and preventative care.
