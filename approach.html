My Story & Approach

This isn't a business venture—it's who I've always been

I've lived health sovereignty my entire life. Not because it became trendy, but because I never bought into the mainstream medical system—even as a teenager.

A Way of Life, Not a Calculated Move

Many people in the "holistic health" space jumped on the bandwagon in their 30s or 40s. I've been here since my teens.

I raised my kids this way. My wife and I had home births with midwives for every single child. We've never relied on the system to tell us what's best for our family—we learned to understand our bodies and make informed decisions ourselves.

I've been "anti-vax" since forever—not because it's trendy now, but because I questioned the narrative decades ago. I've spent my entire career touching, tasting, and understanding herbs firsthand—not just prescribing them from a book.

This is the difference between someone who genuinely lives this way and someone who calculated it as a business opportunity. I'm not here to cash in. I'm here to share what I've lived and learned my entire life with people ready to take responsibility for their own health.

Education over prescription

You'll understand why something is recommended, how it works in your body, and what to watch for—so you can make informed decisions.

Hands-on, lived experience

I've touched, tasted, and worked with these herbs for decades. This isn't book learning—it's genuine connection to the medicine.

Real support, not automation

This isn't a system designed to shuttle you through. It's about real human care and teaching you to become sovereign in your health.

Tools we rely on

  • Classical acupuncture + electro-acupuncture for chronic pain, sleep, and regulation.
  • Functional medicine testing (GI-MAP, DUTCH, metabolic labs) when data clarifies the next step.
  • NeuroClarity homework: breath, vagus training, and light movement to reinforce each visit.
  • Telemedicine + WellPath Community for check-ins, education, and accountability.
