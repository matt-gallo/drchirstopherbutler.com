This Is About Education, Not Just Treatment

You're not here to have everything done for you. You're here to learn how your body works and take responsibility for your health.

I've practiced health sovereignty my entire life—decades of hands-on experience with herbs, raising my family outside the mainstream medical system, and understanding the body through lived experience, not just book learning. This work is about:

  • Understanding what your symptoms are actually telling you
  • Learning to make informed decisions instead of outsourcing to a system
  • Committing to the daily work required for real transformation
  • Building your capacity to read your own signals
  • Chiseling away what doesn't serve you—ongoing, not a destination

What to Expect

This isn't a place where you show up, get needled, and leave thinking the problem is solved. Real healing requires your active participation.

Some people see dramatic improvements quickly. Others take months or even years—because the human body is complex. But regardless of how long it takes, you have to show up consistently and do what needs to be done.

If you're looking for someone to helicopter you to the top of the mountain, this isn't it. I'm here to guide you, support you, and teach you—but you still have to climb.

If you're not ready to commit, please save us both the time. But if you are, let's work together.
