Functional Medicine

Deep dives for complex cases

We investigate gut health, hormones, blood sugar, brain function, and immune markers to build precise plans.

Who it's for

Brain fog, chronic fatigue, anxiety, fertility planning, autoimmune flares, metabolic changes, and long-haul recovery.

Testing options

GI-MAP, DUTCH, micronutrient, metabolic, inflammatory, and neuroendocrine labs—ordered only when results change the plan.

Deliverables

Step-by-step protocols covering nutrition, supplementation, sleep, nervous system work, and follow-up checkpoints.

What a consult includes

  • 60-minute telemedicine or in-person intake
  • Lab review + interpretation
  • Written plan with priorities, metrics, and timelines

Ready to start?

Book a new patient slot and note “functional consult.” We’ll confirm any labs needed beforehand.
