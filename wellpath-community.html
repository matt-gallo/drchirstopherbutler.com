Common questions

Is the community virtual?

Yes. Members join from all over the world. If you need hands-on care, we coordinate intensives through the Cranford clinic.

What’s the commitment?

It’s month-to-month. You can cancel anytime, though most stay because they value how steady they feel with support.

How much time will it take?

Use it like a gym membership. Some months you’ll be in every call, others you’ll lurk, catch replays, and revisit protocols.

Is this different from clinic care?

Clinic work is for complex cases needing private treatment plans. The community is for ongoing guidance, protocols, and belonging.

What happens when I join?

You’ll get instant access to the Skool portal, orientation path, and live call calendar. Introduce yourself and we’ll point you to the right threads.

Can I expense this?

Many members use HSA/FSA funds. We’ll provide receipts and documentation if your plan requires it.
